Teaching Philosophy

My teaching approach emphasizes the practical application of social psychology principles to real-world business and organizational contexts. I believe in fostering critical thinking and encouraging students to question assumptions about emotional communication and social perception.

Student Supervision

I actively supervise both MA and PhD students conducting research in social perception, emotional communication, and organizational behavior. If you're interested in graduate research in these areas, please see the contact page.
