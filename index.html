About Me

Professor Shlomo Hareli is a social psychologist focused on studying emotions at the University of Haifa's School of Business Administration. He serves as the Head of the Laboratory for the Study of Social Perception of Emotions and holds a faculty appointment as a Professor in the School of Business Administration.

His research program aims to reveal mysteries around the social communication function of emotions, with work conducted by his laboratory spanning diverse research topics and extensive empirical investigations.

Research Interests

Professor Hareli's research focuses on the social signal value and communication function of emotion expressions. His work spans the following key areas:

1. Mapping Social Information from Emotions

Studying the types of social information people derive from observing emotions and their impact on domains like achievement, motivation, and relationships.

2. Emotion Expression Dynamics and Social Interactions

Examining how emotion expression dynamics and unfolding social interactions impact emotion perception. Developed an influential "Frozen Dynamism" methodology for examining emotional expressions over time in controlled experiments.

3. Context Effects on Emotion Perception

Examining the influence of context on emotion perception both empirically and theoretically. Made key contributions including identifying bidirectional relationships between context and emotions, and determining that different emotions have varying susceptibility to contextual factors ("situative informativeness").

4. Personality Trait Inferences from Emotions

Advancing understanding of how inferences about personality traits like modesty, arrogance, and dominance are drawn from emotional expressions and reactions.

5. Graduate Student Training

Training graduate students to conduct pioneering studies related to the social perception of emotions and environmental influences on emotional perception and social judgment.

6. Theoretical Frameworks and Scholarly Contribution

Publishing widely-cited theoretical frameworks, comprehensive reviews, and edited books to shape the field around emotion communication and social-functional accounts of emotional expression.

7. AI Applications in Emotion Research

Developing procedures for leveraging AI in emotion perception research to generate and validate experiences and testing dynamic aspects of social perception of emotions through advanced computational methods.
