My Facebook Group

Closed Group for Students and Colleagues

I maintain a closed Facebook group dedicated to sharing research updates, academic discussions, and engaging content related to social psychology and emotions.

Group Description

A closed group of former students of mine as well as interested friends and colleagues who receive news about my own research, but mainly about new research in social psychology and related fields as well as talks from leading scholars in the field. Members can post relevant information and ask questions about these topics.

The group serves as a collaborative space where members stay informed about:

  • Recent research findings from Prof. Hareli's laboratory
  • New publications in social psychology and emotion research
  • Invited presentations and talks from leading scholars
  • Discussions and questions related to emotion perception and social psychology
  • Relevant academic and professional updates

Featured Talks and Presentations in the Group

Below are selected talks and presentations that have been featured and discussed within the group:

Emotional Intelligence: From Theory to Everyday Practice
Discussion of how emotional intelligence concepts translate to real-world applications in personal and professional life.
The Lethality of Loneliness: John Cacioppo
Featuring research on the profound health impacts of loneliness and social isolation, and the biological mechanisms underlying social connection.
Dunning-Kruger Effect
Analysis of the cognitive bias where people with limited knowledge or competence overestimate their abilities, and its implications for social judgment.
Secrets of Attraction – A Talk by Prof. Karl Grammer
Insights into the psychological and behavioral factors that influence human attraction and mate selection.
Science of Persuasion
Exploration of the psychological principles underlying effective persuasion and influence in communication and decision-making.

How to Join

This is a closed, invite-only group designed for:

  • Former graduate and undergraduate students
  • Colleagues and collaborators in the field
  • Researchers interested in social psychology and emotions
  • Friends with shared interests in academic discussions

If you are interested in joining the group, please contact Prof. Hareli directly at shareli@univ.haifa.ac.il.
