Dissemination Activities

Prof. Hareli's presentations address various aspects of social psychology and the perception of emotions, with content tailored to each institution's needs and interests. Topics include:

  • The social signal value of emotions in organizational contexts
  • How emotional expressions influence social judgments and interpersonal dynamics
  • The role of context in emotion perception and interpretation
  • Personality trait inferences from emotional expressions
  • Applications of emotion research in leadership, healthcare, and organizational behavior
  • Training in emotional intelligence and social perception skills

These dissemination activities aim to share academic research with practitioners and professionals in various fields, facilitating knowledge transfer and practical application of emotion perception research.
