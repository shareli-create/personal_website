Posters Presented at Conferences

Below is a selection of poster presentations from major academic conferences and events where Prof. Hareli and colleagues have shared their research findings.

Note: PDF downloads for conference posters are temporarily unavailable. For access to specific poster files, please contact Prof. Hareli at [email protected]

Poster Presentations by Conference

18th General Meeting of the European Association of Social Psychology
Granada, 2017
"The influence of another person's emotion expressions on situation perception"
Presenters: Ursula Hess & Shlomo Hareli

This poster presented research on how observing others' emotional reactions influences the observer's perception and interpretation of the situation itself, demonstrating the bidirectional relationship between emotion perception and contextual interpretation.

International Society for Research on Emotion
St. Louis, 2017
"How the situative informativeness of emotions affects social inferences drawn from them – The case of awe and happiness"
Presenter: Shlomo Hareli

This work examined how different emotions vary in their susceptibility to contextual factors. The poster focused on awe and happiness as case studies, exploring how situative informativeness—the degree to which an emotion communicates information about the situation—affects social judgments.

The 7th Consortium of European Research on Emotion (CERE)
Glasgow, 2018
"The role of emotion expression in health perception"
Presenter: Shlomo Hareli

This research explored how facial expressions of emotions influence observers' judgments about a person's health status and well-being, contributing to understanding of non-verbal communication in health and medical contexts.

About Our Conference Presentations

Prof. Hareli and his laboratory team regularly present their research findings at major international conferences including:

  • European Association of Social Psychology (EASP)
  • International Society for Research on Emotion (ISRE)
  • Consortium of European Research on Emotion (CERE)
  • Various psychology and emotion research conferences worldwide

These presentations showcase cutting-edge research on the social perception of emotions, contributing to the advancement of the field and fostering international collaboration among emotion researchers.
