Research Datasets

Haifa Set of Facial Expressions of Emotions (HSFEE)

The HSFEE is a comprehensive database of facial expressions developed for emotion perception research. The dataset includes validated expressions of basic emotions photographed under controlled conditions.

Dataset Features:

  • Multiple emotion categories
  • Professional photography
  • Validated expressions
  • Available for research use

Body Shape and Expressions Dataset

AI-generated images exploring the intersection of body language and emotional expression. Two versions available with different methodological approaches.

Access Information:

For access to these datasets, please contact Prof. Hareli via the contact page with details about your research project.
